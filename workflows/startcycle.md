## Workflow Steps

Initialize Specs: Call write_specs to define the 2026 "Liquid Glass" design system and election scenarios.

Execute Build: Hand off specs to generate_code to build the Next.js frontend and gamified timeline.

Refinement Loop: Run audit_code to ensure the Glassmorphism isn't breaking legibility and that the "Voter Practice Ballot" works flawlessly.

Production: Execute deploy_app for the final launch.
